MY ACHIEVEMENT


my achievement will surely reflect at the time when I was called upon by my authority school to participate in an inter school debate and quiz competition.

I was really timid because i did not have the courage to face the audience. But wih the help of God in my life, I was able overcome such outrageous courage.

it was a normal routine for me to stay behind after classes for the tutorial and encouragement for the competition.

the day had already come when I woke up as early as possible in the mid-night to practise somethings which I considered could be the challenge.

i was absolutely confident about this competition.

During the tutorial earlier, i picked the two words from the teacher which were COURAGEOUS and COMMITTMENT.

when i got to the venue, i was intimidated by the way the judges were looking like but with time i shrugged it off.

when it was my turn,i took the bull by its horn and when straight to the point. The jugdes were impressed with the way i composed myself.

At the end of the debates, the judges were full of my praises and eventually gave me my reward which was a gold medal and the cup for my school.

That is that about my Life Achievement.
